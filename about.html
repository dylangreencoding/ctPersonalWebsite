Before getting into web development, I worked in several small businesses. I came to know well the daily struggle that every small business owner faces. This is what has inspired me to do what I do. It is more than just my job, it is what I enjoy doing.

Aside from my work, I enjoy good conversation, hiking, climbing, travelling, and learning new things.
